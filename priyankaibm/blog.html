What is Extensive Farming?

  • Mani
  • Jan 12, 2021
  • 12 Comments

Extensive farming, also known as extensive agriculture, is a method of farming that involves using large areas of land to cultivate crops or raise livestock with relatively low inputs of labor, capital, and technology per unit of land. This approach contrasts with intensive farming, which focuses on maximizing production within a smaller land area through higher inputs and more advanced practices.

About AgriTech

Agritech, short for "agricultural technology", refers to the use of advanced technology and innovations to enhance various aspects of agriculture. Agritech encompasses a wide range of technologies and solutions that aim to improve efficiency, productivity, and sustainability in the agricultural sector. These technologies can range from software applications and data analytics to hardware devices and machinery.
